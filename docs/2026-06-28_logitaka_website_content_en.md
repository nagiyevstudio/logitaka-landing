   - *Answer:* Logitaka is a voice-first cognitive exoskeleton and project operating system designed for professionals who manage high cognitive loads. It takes raw, unstructured voice recordings or text notes and formats them into organized tasks, calendar events, and project roadmaps.

   - *Answer:* It is simple: you press record and dictate your raw thoughts, project updates, or meeting minutes. Behind the scenes, Logitaka's structured engine processes the audio, extracts tasks and decisions, schedules deadlines, and updates your project space in seconds.

   - *Answer:* Logitaka is built specifically for Solopreneurs, Product Managers, Consultants, Tech Leads, and Deep Focus professionals who need to manage complex information streams and cannot afford to lose project context.
