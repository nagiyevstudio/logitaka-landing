## 5. What must be included in Privacy Policy

The page must include these sections:

1. `Who We Are`
2. `What Information We Collect`
3. `How We Use Information`
4. `AI Processing`
5. `Payments`
6. `How We Share Information`
7. `Data Retention`
8. `Data Security`
9. `International Processing`
10. `Your Rights`
11. `Children’s Privacy`
12. `Cookies`
13. `Changes to This Privacy Policy`
14. `Contact`

### Special instructions for Privacy
Do **not** say “we only collect name and email.”  
Use a truthful and careful formulation like this:

#### What information we collect
Include these categories:
- account information:
  - name
  - email address
  - login/authentication-related information
- user content:
  - projects
  - notes
  - tasks
  - documents
  - messages
  - uploaded content
  - AI prompts and generated outputs
- technical/service data:
  - essential session/authentication data
  - IP address
  - browser/device basics
  - security and error logs necessary to operate and protect the service
- payment-related information:
  - payment status
  - payment references/confirmation information received from the payment provider
  - explicitly say full card/payment details are processed by Kapital Bank or its payment systems, not by Logitaka

#### How data is used
Include:
- provide the service
- authenticate users
- store and organize user content
- support AI features requested by the user
- provide support
- process payments/subscription access
- protect service security
- investigate problems and errors
- comply with legal obligations

#### Sharing
List only categories that are real:
- hosting/infrastructure providers
- email delivery providers
- AI service providers
- payment service providers
- legal/compliance disclosures where required by law

Do **not** mention analytics vendors.

#### Hosting / international processing
State that infrastructure or providers may process data outside Azerbaijan, including in Germany, where necessary for service operation.

#### Retention
Use this logic:
- account data and user content are retained while the account remains active and the service is being used
- data may be deleted upon user request
- limited technical and debugging records may be retained for short periods where necessary for troubleshooting and security
- AI debug artifacts may be retained for up to `72 hours` and then deleted automatically

#### Cookies
State clearly:
- only essential cookies required for authentication, sessions, and secure operation
- no advertising cookies
- no analytics cookies

#### User rights
Keep it simple:
- access
- correction
- deletion
- support contact
- export available on request

- not overly short
- readable for normal users

- promises of “absolute security”
- promises that no technical logs exist
- promises that no third parties ever process user data
- promises of automatic renewals or recurring billing
- promises of self-service data export if it does not exist
