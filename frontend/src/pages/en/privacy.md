## 2. What Information We Collect
We collect only the information necessary to provide and secure the Service. This includes:

### Account Information
* Name and email address;
* Login and authentication-related records.

### User Content
* Projects, tasks, notes, documents, messages, and uploaded files;
* AI prompts and instructions you submit;
* Generated outputs and summaries.

### Technical and Service Data
* Essential session and authentication data (cookies);
* IP address and basic device/browser information;
* Security and error logs required to operate and protect the Service.

### Payment-related Information
* Payment status and reference/confirmation data received from the payment provider.
* **Note:** Logitaka does **not** receive or store full card or payment instrument details. All such data is processed directly by **Kapital Bank** or its authorized payment systems.

## 3. How We Use Information
We use your data to:
* Provide and maintain the Service;
* Authenticate users and secure accounts;
* Store, organize, and display your content;
* Support AI-assisted features requested by you;
* Process payments and manage subscription access;
* Provide customer support;
* Investigate technical problems and errors;
* Protect the Service against abuse, fraud, and security incidents;
* Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. AI Processing
When you use AI features, your prompts and related project context are processed by third-party AI providers to generate results. We use services from providers including:
* OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, x.ai, z.ai, Mistral, and Kimi.

Technical and diagnostic records related to AI requests may be retained for up to **72 hours** to ensure reliability and then are automatically deleted.

## 5. Payments
Payments are handled manually through payment links. All financial transactions are processed by our payment provider, **Kapital Bank**. We do not store your full card details on our servers.

## 6. How We Share Information
We do not sell your data. We share information only with service providers necessary for the operation of Logitaka:
* **Infrastructure:** Hetzner (servers located in Nuremberg, Germany);
* **Email Delivery:** supremecluster.com;
* **AI Providers:** As listed in Section 4;
* **Payment Services:** Kapital Bank;
* **Legal Disclosures:** Where required by law or lawful government request.

We do **not** use third-party analytics or marketing trackers.

## 7. Data Retention
* Account data and user content are retained as long as your account remains active and you continue to use the Service.
* We process deletion requests promptly. Upon request, your account and related stored data will be deleted from our active systems.
* Limited technical logs and debugging records are kept for short periods as necessary for troubleshooting and security.
* AI debug artifacts are deleted automatically after 72 hours.

## 8. Data Security
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

## 9. International Processing
Our infrastructure and providers may process data outside your country of residence, including in **Germany** (Hetzner infrastructure) and the United States, where necessary for the operation of the Service.
